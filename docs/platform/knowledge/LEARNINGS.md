### DSR: Deflated Sharpe Ratio for Multiple Testing Correction

## Pattern: Deflated Sharpe Ratio (DSR)

When testing N strategy variants, the best Sharpe ratio is biased upward. DSR (Bailey & Lopez de Prado, 2014) corrects for this by computing the probability that the observed Sharpe exceeds the expected maximum Sharpe under the null hypothesis of zero skill.
